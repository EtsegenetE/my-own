Lasagna Recipe

Our hotel perpared this recipe menu to make clear of how we make our mojitos.

Ingredients

  • 3 sliced lemon
  • 2 cup of sugar
  • 3cup of bacardi
  • 1 sprite
  • anubias barteri

How to use the ingredients

  1. mix the anubias barteri and lemon well
  2. add 2 cups of sugar to it
  3. pour the bacardi and sprite at once
  4. put the lemon and anubias at the top and serve
